Calculation and audit details

Annualized basis: average all included representative gross checks, multiply by annual pay periods, then convert the annualized result into an average month and an estimated 30-day period.

  • Weekly: average check × 52
  • Bi-weekly: average check × 26
  • Semi-monthly: average check × 24
  • Monthly: average check × 12

Actual prior-30-day basis: count all entered checks with pay dates inside the as-of-date window, plus other income explicitly entered as received during that window.

Older paystubs remain usable for projection. The widget reports their age and warns staff to confirm continued employment or wage changes. Both basis results and the historical earned amount remain in the audit record regardless of the selected primary basis.

Optional AMI uses average monthly projected income when the annualized basis is selected, or the actual prior-30-day received amount as the monthly proxy when that basis is selected.
